## Original (English)

### Headline

**Department of War Releases Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena Files in Historic Transparency Effort**

Source: U.S. Department of War — Release dated May 8, 2026.

### Summary (synthesized from cross-corroborated reporting)

On May 8, 2026, the U.S. Department of War (the renamed Department of Defense) announced the initial release of formerly classified files on Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAP) under the **Presidential Unsealing and Reporting System for UAP Encounters (PURSUE)** program. The release marks the opening phase of a multi-agency declassification effort directed by President Donald J. Trump in February 2026.

The first tranche, posted publicly to **https://www.war.gov/UFO/**, contains **162 records — 120 PDF documents, 28 videos, and 14 images** — drawn from holdings of the Federal Bureau of Investigation, the Department of War (formerly Department of Defense), the National Aeronautics and Space Administration, and the Department of State. The records span incidents from 1947 through 2026 and are described by the Department as "unresolved." Of the 162 files, **108 contain redactions**, which the Department says were applied "to protect the identity of eyewitnesses, the location of government facilities, or potentially sensitive information about military sites not related to UAP."

### Key Quote

> "The Department of War is in lockstep with President Trump to bring unprecedented transparency regarding our government's understanding of Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena."
> — **Pete Hegseth**, Secretary of War

### Notable Files in the First Release

- An **Apollo 17** photograph from December 1972, reportedly showing three dots in a triangular formation in the lunar sky.
- An updated, less-redacted version of the FBI file **62-HQ-83894**, documenting UFO-related reports between 1947 and 1968.
- An internal military memo describing "**one possible small UAP**" observed in **Iraq in 2022**.
- A report describing "**multiple glares or light from an unknown origin**" observed in **Syria in 2024**.
- A **2023** federal-law-enforcement incident report describing **unusual orange "orbs"** that appeared to emit smaller reddish spheres — characterized internally as among the more compelling cases held by the All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office (AARO) due to witness credibility.

### Program Mechanics

PURSUE is described by the Department as an interagency program established after the President directed the Department of War in February 2026 to "find, review, identify, declassify and publicly release unresolved UAP-related records and historical documents in the federal government's possession." Officials stated that further tranches will be added to the war.gov/UFO portal **on a rolling basis** in the coming weeks and months.

### Congressional Response

Representative **Anna Paulina Luna** characterized the May 8 release as "a great first step," and noted that a second tranche — expected to include previously requested video footage — could arrive within weeks.

### Open Questions Flagged by Outside Observers

Reporting from The Debrief and Liberation Times, both UAP-focused outlets, raised that (i) the May 8 set is heavy on legacy FBI material and Apollo-era imagery whose UAP relevance is debated, and (ii) the rolling-release format does not yet include the specific cockpit-video and sensor-data items previously sought by congressional task forces. AARO holdings of high-resolution sensor data remain, as of this filing, only partially represented.

## 简体中文译稿

### 标题

**美国战争部历史性透明化行动：公开"不明异常现象"（UAP）档案**

来源：美国战争部（U.S. Department of War）——2026 年 5 月 8 日新闻稿。

### 摘要（多源交叉核对合成）

2026 年 5 月 8 日，美国战争部（U.S. Department of War，原国防部 Department of Defense 改组而来）宣布，根据**总统不明异常现象遭遇启封与报告系统（Presidential Unsealing and Reporting System for UAP Encounters，PURSUE）**计划，首批此前列为机密的"不明异常现象"（Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena, UAP）档案对外公开。本次公开是 2026 年 2 月由唐纳德·J·特朗普（Donald J. Trump）总统下令、由战争部牵头协调的跨机构解密行动的**首阶段**。

首批档案上传至公共门户网站 **https://www.war.gov/UFO/**，共 **162 件——其中 PDF 文档 120 件、视频 28 件、图像 14 件**——分别来自联邦调查局（FBI）、战争部（原国防部）、美国国家航空航天局（NASA）以及国务院（Department of State）的馆藏。档案涉及的事件时间跨度从 1947 年延伸至 2026 年，战争部均将其归类为"未结案（unresolved）"。在 162 件文件中，**108 件含有涂黑（redaction）**，战争部称涂黑用于"保护目击者身份、政府设施位置，或与 UAP 无关但仍敏感的军事站点信息"。

### 重点引语

> "战争部与特朗普总统步调一致，致力于在政府对'不明异常现象'的了解方面实现前所未有的透明度。"
> ——战争部部长 **皮特·海格塞斯（Pete Hegseth）**

### 首批公开的代表性文件

- 1972 年 12 月**阿波罗 17 号（Apollo 17）**任务期间拍摄的一张照片，据报显示在月球上空有三个呈三角形排列的光点。
- 联邦调查局档案 **62-HQ-83894** 的最新版本，记录 1947—1968 年间的 UFO 相关报告，相比此前版本涂黑减少。
- 一份军方内部备忘录，描述 **2022 年伊拉克**境内观察到"**一个可能的小型 UAP**"。
- 一份报告，描述 **2024 年叙利亚**境内观察到"**来源不明的多次眩光或光体**"。
- **2023 年**一份联邦执法人员的事件报告，描述了**异常的橙色"球体"（orbs）**，并称这些球体似乎能释放出更小的红色球体——被内部评定为全域异常解析办公室（All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office, AARO）所持有的较具说服力的案例之一，原因是目击者的身份可信度高。

### 计划运作方式

战争部表示，PURSUE 是一项跨机构计划，2026 年 2 月由总统指示战争部"快速查找、审阅、识别、解密并公开发布联邦政府持有的未解 UAP 相关档案与历史文件"后设立。官员表示，后续批次将在未来数周与数月内**滚动式（on a rolling basis）**陆续上传至 war.gov/UFO 门户。

### 国会回应

众议员 **安娜·保利娜·卢娜（Anna Paulina Luna）**称 5 月 8 日的此次发布为"很好的第一步"，并表示包括此前要求的视频素材在内的第二批档案有望在数周内推出。

### 外部观察者提出的疑问

UAP 垂直媒体 The Debrief 与 Liberation Times 的报道指出：（一）5 月 8 日批次中，FBI 旧案与阿波罗时期影像占比较高，其与 UAP 主题的相关性仍存争议；（二）滚动公开的格式目前尚未涵盖国会工作组此前重点索取的座舱视频与传感器数据。截至本档案归档时，AARO 持有的高分辨率传感器资料仅有部分被纳入此次首批公开。
